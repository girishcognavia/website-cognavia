## What is CognaBrief?

CognaBrief is an intelligent document summarization platform designed for professionals, researchers, and organizations who need to quickly extract key information from large volumes of documents. Whether you're reviewing research papers, legal documents, technical reports, or business proposals, CognaBrief delivers comprehensive summaries that preserve critical details while saving you hours of reading time.

Our platform combines cutting-edge AI technology with a sophisticated quality assurance system to ensure every summary meets professional standards. Unlike basic summarization tools, CognaBrief understands document structure, extracts metadata, references figures and tables, and provides page-level citations for complete traceability.

## Key Features

### Intelligent Document Processing

- **Multi-Format Support**: Upload PDF, DOCX, and TXT files up to 10MB
- **Advanced Text Extraction**: Accurately extracts text from complex documents including multi-column layouts and scanned pages
- **Metadata Recognition**: Automatically identifies authors, publication dates, DOI references, and document structure
- **Visual Element Detection**: Recognizes and references figures, tables, and charts within your documents

### Three Summary Levels

Choose the depth of analysis that fits your needs:

| Summary Type | Description | Ideal For |
|-------------|-------------|-----------|
| **Quick** | Concise 2-3 sentence overview with 5 key bullet points (~150 words) | Rapid document triage, email briefings |
| **Standard** | Comprehensive summary with methodology, findings, and conclusions (~500 words) | Team presentations, stakeholder updates |
| **Detailed** | In-depth analysis covering all major sections with full citations (~1500 words) | Research reviews, due diligence, deep analysis |

### Dual-AI Quality Assurance

CognaBrief employs a unique dual-AI architecture that sets it apart from conventional summarization tools:

1. **Primary AI Engine**: Generates professional summaries using state-of-the-art language models trained on academic and professional content
2. **Quality Review Agent**: An independent AI system that evaluates every summary against six rigorous criteria, ensuring consistent quality

This two-stage process means your summaries are never just generatedâ€”they're verified, refined, and approved before delivery.

### Professional Output Format

Every summary includes:

- **Document Header**: Original filename, file type, size, page count, and processing metadata
- **Executive Summary**: Three-sentence overview capturing the essence of the document
- **At-a-Glance Section**: Five critical takeaways with specific data points
- **Structured Content**: Organized sections covering background, findings, methodology, and conclusions
- **Page Citations**: Every claim linked to its source page for easy verification
- **Metrics Footer**: Word count comparison, compression ratio, and estimated time saved

## How It Works

### Step 1: Upload Your Document

Simply drag and drop your document into the upload area or click to browse your files. CognaBrief accepts:
- PDF documents (including scanned with OCR support)
- Microsoft Word documents (DOCX)
- Plain text files (TXT)

### Step 2: Select Summary Type

Choose from Quick, Standard, or Detailed summaries based on your needs. Each type is optimized for different use cases and time constraints.

### Step 3: AI Processing

Once you initiate summarization, CognaBrief's AI engine:
1. Analyzes document structure and content
2. Extracts key information, statistics, and findings
3. Identifies figures, tables, and visual elements
4. Generates a professionally formatted summary
5. Runs quality assurance checks
6. Refines the summary if needed (up to 3 iterations)

### Step 4: Review and Export

Access your completed summary with:
- Full text view with professional formatting
- Copy to clipboard functionality
- Download as text file
- Compression statistics showing time saved

## Quality Assurance System

CognaBrief's intelligent review agent evaluates every summary against six key criteria:

### Evaluation Criteria

| Criterion | Weight | What It Measures |
|-----------|--------|------------------|
| **Completeness** | 30% | Coverage of all major topics and findings |
| **Accuracy** | 25% | Factual correctness and data precision |
| **Citations** | 20% | Proper page references for all claims |
| **Visual References** | 10% | Mentions of figures and tables |
| **Structure** | 10% | Logical flow and organization |
| **Critical Information** | 5% | Preservation of key insights |

### Quality Thresholds

- **Excellent (90-100)**: Approved immediately
- **Good (75-89)**: Approved with confidence
- **Below 75**: Automatically refined with specific feedback

This system ensures consistent, professional-quality output regardless of document complexity.

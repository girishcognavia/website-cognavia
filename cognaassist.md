## What is CognaAssist?

CognaAssist is a SaaS (Software as a Service) platform that provides businesses with an AI-powered chatbot capable of understanding and answering customer questions using the content from your own website. Unlike generic chatbots that rely on pre-programmed responses, CognaAssist uses advanced Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG) technology to provide contextually relevant, accurate answers drawn directly from your website's information.

The platform automatically crawls your website, understands your content, and creates a knowledge base that powers intelligent conversations with your visitors. Whether you run an e-commerce store, a service business, or an information portal, CognaAssist adapts to your specific content and brand voice.

## Technology Behind CognaAssist

### Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG)
CognaAssist uses RAG technology, which combines the power of large language models with your specific business knowledge. When a customer asks a question, the system:

1. Searches your indexed content for relevant information
2. Retrieves the most pertinent passages
3. Generates a natural, accurate response using AI
4. Delivers the answer in a conversational format

This approach ensures responses are always grounded in your actual content, reducing the risk of incorrect or hallucinated information.

### Vector Search Technology
Customer questions are converted into mathematical representations (vectors) and matched against your indexed content using similarity search. This enables semantic understanding, finding relevant content even when customers use different words than your website.

### Intelligent Text Processing
Your website content is processed using advanced natural language processing techniques. Content is cleaned, chunked into optimal segments, and indexed for fast, accurate retrieval.

### Secure Cloud Infrastructure
The platform runs on secure, scalable cloud infrastructure with automatic backups, encryption, and redundancy. Your data is protected and always available.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How quickly can I get started?
Most businesses are up and running within 24-48 hours. The registration review process typically takes less than a day, and the website crawling completes within hours depending on your site's size.

### Does CognaAssist work with any website?
Yes, CognaAssist works with any website that allows JavaScript. It's compatible with all major platforms including WordPress, Shopify, Wix, Squarespace, custom-built sites, and more.

### What languages does CognaAssist support?
CognaAssist primarily supports English content. Multilingual support is planned for future releases.

### How accurate are the responses?
CognaAssist only provides answers based on your actual website content. If information isn't on your site, the chatbot will politely indicate it doesn't have that information, rather than making something up.

### Can I see what customers are asking?
Yes, the admin dashboard provides complete conversation history and analytics. You can see all interactions, common questions, and identify areas where your content might need improvement.

### Is my data secure?
Absolutely. All data is encrypted, stored in isolated environments per tenant, and protected by enterprise-grade security measures. We never share your data with other customers or third parties.

### What if I need to update my website content?
You can request a re-crawl at any time to update the chatbot's knowledge base. For major changes, the system can be configured to automatically refresh periodically.

### Can I customize the chatbot's personality?
Yes, you can customize the greeting message, response style, and visual appearance to match your brand voice and design.

### What happens if the chatbot can't answer a question?
If CognaAssist doesn't have information to answer a question accurately, it will politely acknowledge this and can suggest contacting your support team directly.
